SkillsUSA School Grants

ARIF is pleased to offer grants to support schools whose students compete in SkillsUSA roofing events. Our goal is simple: help grow the next generation of roofing professionals by making competition more accessible.

Eligibility

Vocational schools, community colleges, and other trade education programs with students competing in SkillsUSA roofing events are encouraged to apply. Grant funds are intended to cover reasonable costs of travel, lodging, and registration for the SkillsUSA National Leadership & Skills Conference (NLSC).

Grant Details

Two $1,000 grants are awarded annually. Funds are remitted directly to the school and must be used for roofing-related competition expenses.

How to Apply

Applications are accepted May through September. Recipients will be announced in [month, year]. Complete the form below to apply.
